F.A.Q

How does it work?

By clicking book now you will be redirected to our booking site, from there set your appointment with the services that best suit your needs and let TLV do the rest!

How long do orders take to deliver?

Guranteed deliveries within 48 hours of your appointment.

What do I need to do to prepare?

TLV shoots properties as is, for best results make sure property exteriors are landscaped, neat and any large tools, toys, or other clutter are not in view. Virtual Decluttering is available, but not effective for very large objects.

What if I need to reschedule?

Reschedules and cancellations within 24 hours are free, no-shows or cancellations after that time period are subject to a $45 fee.

Where is TLV available?

We currently shoot real estate in Las Vegas, Henderson, Pahrump and sorrounding areas.
